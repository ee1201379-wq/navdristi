Women Support Section

Fast-track safety and harassment reporting.

Women complaints can be filed from the citizen side under the Women Support Desk and are marked for 1-hour escalation with superior notification.

Male Support Section

Male users get a separate support route for abuse, intimidation, blackmail, and violence complaints without mixing them with normal civic issues.

Elder Emergency Section

Emergency reporting for senior citizens living alone.

This section is for elderly users above 70 who need fast support for falls, medical distress, threats, or living-alone emergencies.
